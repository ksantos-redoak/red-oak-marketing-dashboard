Attribution coverage caveat. Source is traced from the opportunity's primary contact's HubSpot Original Traffic Source, falling back to any associated contact when the primary has none. This resolves 91% of new-business opps (21 of 224 remain unsourceable: 16 have a contact role but no source on the contact, 5 have no contact role at all). The opportunity-level source fields in Salesforce are only ~8% populated and are not used here. Treat the "no source" bucket as unattributed, not as zero-influence.

Contact-role coverage caveat. Influence links opportunities to campaigns through opportunity contact roles, which exist on only 24% of YTD opps (278 of 1,159 total; the build guide flags ~2,901 deals with zero contacts org-wide). This is a "touched-any-influencing-campaign" view, so an opp can be credited to multiple campaigns and the bars do not sum to total pipeline. Real influence is almost certainly understated until contact roles are enforced.
